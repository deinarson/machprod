Stereo Test #1 of 3 Solarized Millennium Garden
Stereo test: A stereoscopic video created with a single video stream. Technique: record one track of video with each frame precisely 6.5cm from the previous. Played back with a delay of one frame to the eye opposite the direction of the motion. A series of video clips exploring esthetic distortions of video, partially inspired by Gianni Toti. Millennium Garden, Nottingham: A video test for a future project involving time-lapse animation.
